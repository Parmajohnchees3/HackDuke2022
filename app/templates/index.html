What is Spirometry?

Most common pulmonary function test (PFT). Measures lung function. Assesses breathing patterns that can help to identify conditions such as
  • Asthma
  • Pulmonary Fibrosis
  • Cystic Fibrosis
  • COPD

Scroll

FVC: volume of air that can forcibly be blown out after full inspiration

FEV: volume of air that can be forcibly be blown out in the first second

FEV1/FVC: ratio between FEV1 and FVC - strong indicator of lung function

FEF: speed of air exiting the lungs
